Wellness Center

Support for Student Well-being

Helps students manage social, personal, and academic challenges effectively.

Safe and Confidential Space

A qualified therapist provides a stigma-free environment for open discussions.

Interaction-Based Approach

No medication, just meaningful conversations to address concerns.

Free and Accessible

Students can avail of hour-long counselling sessions at no cost.

Encouraging Growth

Talking about problems fosters maturity, better decision-making, and personal development.

Peer Support

Guidance from senior students trained in mentoring and emotional support.

Mental Health Awareness

Workshops and sessions to educate students about mental health and stress management.

Academic Assistance

Help with time management, study strategies, and overcoming academic pressure.

Faculty Interaction

Opportunities for students to interact with professors for guidance and mentorship.
